HSgB ROCK

Rx On-call Calculation Kit for Pharmacists

📋 Disclaimer & Terms of Use

Please read carefully before proceeding

HSgB Rx-Oncall Calculation Kit (ROCK) is developed to support HSgB pharmacists during on-call duties by providing quick, evidence-based clinical calculations at the point of care.

ROCK is a clinical support tool only and does not replace clinical assessment & professional judgement.

Users Are Required To:

  • Independently verify all calculations before clinical use
  • Interpret outputs within the appropriate patient context
  • Apply professional judgement before acting on any output
⚠️ As ROCK is newly developed, minor errors may occur. Always verify results before clinical use and report any discrepancies to the development team.
INTERNAL USE ONLY Intended exclusively for HSgB staff. Unauthorised use or distribution is strictly prohibited.
The developers are not responsible for any clinical decisions or outcomes arising from the use or misuse of ROCK.
