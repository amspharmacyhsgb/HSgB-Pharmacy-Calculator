💊 MPRx — Medication Possession Ratio Calculator for Pharmacists

Estimates patient adherence based on pharmacy dispensing records over a defined observation period.

Formula: MPR (%) = (Total Days' Supply Dispensed ÷ Total Days in Observation Period) × 100
Interpretation (WHO):  ✔ Optimal ≥ 90% ⚠ Fair 80–89% ✕ Poor < 80%

🔬 APRI Score — AST to Platelet Ratio Index

Estimates the likelihood of significant hepatic fibrosis in patients with Hepatitis C. For use in MTAC RVD clinical assessment prior to initiating Hep C treatment.

Formula: APRI = [(AST ÷ AST ULN) × 100] ÷ Platelet count (×10⁹/L)
Reference: Malaysian Consensus Guideline on Antiretroviral Therapy 2022.
